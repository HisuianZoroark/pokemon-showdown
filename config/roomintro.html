Trash Channel Original Programming:-
GEN 8!! Bitch and Beggar - Pokémon can 'Beggar-Evolve' using low (<=300) BST Pokémon as Stones.
Mix and Meta - Pit sets from a variety of popular OMs against each other to see which is the strongest. (Check compatible metas by using /om mixandmeta).
BEA5T M0D3 - Pokémon in this ZU-based meta can set other species as moves (including Ubers and Mega formes) to transform into them.
The Call of Pikacthulhu - Pokémon have Perish status applied when entering battle.

Mirror Universe Mashups:-
Suicide Cup: Random Battle - Play Suicide Cup with randomly-generated viable sets
CRABmons - Pokémon change type to match any two moves they could learn naturally, and can use any move of their new typing, in addition to the moves they can normally learn.
